\section{Abstract}
Entropy-bounded recursive systems often peak at an intermediate depth. We frame this behavior through two depth profiles---a coherence term $S_\phi(d)$ with a mode near $\varphi^2$ and a stability term $S_\kappa(d)$ with a mode in the $3$--$4$ range---whose product $\ell(d)$ yields a single maximizer $d^*$. This note summarizes the simulation protocols used to explore that hypothesis.
